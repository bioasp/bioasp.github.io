Who we are

BioASP is developed by several people at several institutions from several countries:

  • DyLISS, CNRS & Inria (Rennes, France)
  • Potassco, Potsdam University (Potsdam, Germany)
  • MeForBio, IRCCyN & CNRS (Nantes, France)
  • ARB, Max Planck Institute (Magdeburg, Germany)
  • Systems Biomedicine, EMBL-EBI (Hinxton, UK)
If you would like to contribute in any way, your are welcome to join us at http://github.com/bioasp.
